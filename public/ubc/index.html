Voter Funded Media at UBC - Jan/Feb 2009

Below are the results of University of British Columbia's Jan/Feb 2009 student election Voter Funded Media contest. Voting took place at adm.ams.ubc.ca/Elections2009/ from January 29 - February 2, and on paper ballots February 4. This was the first-ever VFM contest sponsored by its voting community! Election voter turnout was the highest in 22 years. See also the UBC Alma Mater Society VFM page.

Contest rules were similar to those in 2007-2008.
